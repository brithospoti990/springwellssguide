About

We help homeowners solve hard water — honestly.

SpringWell SS Guide is an independent resource for homeowners dealing with hard water, scale, and whole-house water quality. Our approach is simple: diagnose the real problem first, then recommend a system only when it genuinely fits. A softener, a filter, and a salt-free conditioner solve different problems — and pretending otherwise is how people end up disappointed. We'd rather send you to the right answer than the most expensive one.

Scott Murley

Founder & lead reviewer

Water-treatment writer and former residential install tech.

I spent years around residential water systems before writing about them full-time — sizing softeners, cutting in bypass valves, and fielding the same homeowner questions over and over: Why are my dishes spotty? Why won't soap rinse off? Do I need a softener or a filter? This site is the resource I wish those homeowners had had: plain-language answers, real cost math, and recommendations that start from your symptoms instead of a sales quota.

How we review

Every recommendation here is built from the same process:

  • Problem first. We map the symptom (scale, taste, smell, staining) to the treatment that actually addresses it before naming any product.
  • Specs verified at the source. Capacities, flow rates, and warranties are checked against manufacturer documentation, not copied from other reviews.
  • Honest trade-offs. Every review includes a clear "who should look elsewhere" — because no system is right for everyone.
  • Real cost of ownership. We account for salt, maintenance, and lifespan, not just the sticker price.
  • Trusted sources for science. We lean on the USGS, EPA, CDC, and NSF/WQA for water-quality and certification facts.

How we make money

This site is reader-supported. When you buy through some of our links, we earn a commission at no additional cost to you. That never changes our recommendation — we point to the system that fits your problem, and we say plainly when a cheaper or different option is the better call. Full details are in our affiliate disclosure.
